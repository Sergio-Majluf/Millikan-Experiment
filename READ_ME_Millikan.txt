#Notes:
# Run millikan_video.py program
# It will ask you to choose video to analyze, find the video on your computer folder

# 1. Left click on the top of the starting position on the grid
# 2. Left click on the bottom after 10 squares from the top line you chose
# 3. Click on the starting position of the particle you want to measure on the video
# 4. Click the starting position of the particle, a yellow square will appear
# 5. Click the finishing position of the particle, a purple circle will appear
# 6. Click to start the timer once the particle crosses the first horizontal bar, click again when it crosses the second horizontal bar
#7 The info about time, and speed will be printed in the command window

# Press q to close program
# Right Mouse Button (RMB) Drag to scroll the view up/down
# Left Mouse Button (LMB) to perform calibration and measurements as per instructions
